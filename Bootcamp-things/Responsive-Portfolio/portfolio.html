About Me

Born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. I attended Southern Illinois University of Edwardsville, and received a Bachelors of Science in Nutrition. Having recently moved down to Texas, I saw an opportunity to take this coding bootcamp and grabbed hold of it. My interests are video games, exercise, and trying new types of food/restaurants.
